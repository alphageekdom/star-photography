About

Hi, I'm Star

As a passionate Latina photographer based in Orange, California, I'm dedicated to capturing the beauty and emotion of every portrait and wedding I shoot. With a keen eye for detail and a genuine love for the art of photography, I create images that resonate with authenticity and warmth.

My journey into photography began with a childhood fascination for capturing fleeting moments — a pursuit that has since evolved into a lifelong dedication to visual storytelling. Whether I'm photographing the tender moments of a wedding day or the joyous smiles of a family, I pour my heart into every shot, creating timeless works of art that celebrate life and love.

— Star
